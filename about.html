About Me



Hi, I am Alexa, a production planner by day and a data scientist by night. I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Information Technology and a Master of Science degree in Data Analytics from Western Governors University. I've always loved solving problems and sharing my insights with others. I'm passionate about using data to make impactful decisions.

I am a production planner with 8 years of experience in the manufacturing industry. I use data science techniques to analyze historical data and forecast demand to construct a viable production schedule. I also use various techniques to optimize the schedules as well as identify any potential bottlenecks.
I am passionate about data and the various ways that it can be manipulated to solve problems within the day-to-day business processes. I am a strong team player and strive to help others.

In my spare time, I work on personal projects using data science to solve real-world problems and topics that pique my interest.

When I am not working or creating projects, you can find me spending time with my family, chasing my corgi around, or watching Thai dramas.
